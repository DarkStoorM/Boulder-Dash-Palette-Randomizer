Boulder Dash Palette Randomizer

This tool creates a palette of random colors for Boulder Dash.

Each color can be locked for the next generation.

By default, all colors are clamped between a certain range of values for all RGB channels (0-255), fine-tuned to give a bit more pleasant palette, avoiding combinations of very bright colors.
